Hydroponics Setup & Materials

Start small and scale up with these affordable material options tailored to different budgets.

1. Basic (₹5,000 – ₹10,000)

  • PVC pipes (or food-grade containers) for channels
  • Net pots and nursery cups
  • Cocopeat or clay pebbles (LECA) as media
  • DIY nutrients (pre-mix powders) and simple timers

2. Medium (₹15,000 – ₹25,000)

  • Submersible water pumps and air pumps with stones
  • pH & EC meter for monitoring solution strength
  • LED grow lights (full-spectrum) for indoor setups
  • Improved reservoirs, tubing, and fittings

3. Advanced (₹30,000+)

  • Automation (controllers, dosing pumps, smart timers)
  • Greenhouse structure or climate control equipment
  • Commercial-grade nutrients and filtration systems
  • Scalable NFT/DWC modules for higher throughput
