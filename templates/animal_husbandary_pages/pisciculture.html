Pisciculture

Good water, right seed, and proper feeding for faster, healthier growth.

Overview

Pisciculture means growing fish in ponds or tanks. Keep water clean, stock healthy seed, and feed on time for better growth and income.

Key Practices

  • Prepare ponds: dry, lime, fertilize as needed.
  • Stock healthy fingerlings at recommended densities.
  • Monitor water quality: oxygen, pH, ammonia, temperature.
  • Feed on schedule; avoid overfeeding to reduce waste.
  • Prevent predators and maintain proper netting.
