Poultry Farming

Efficient housing, balanced feed, and strict biosecurity for healthy flocks.

Overview

Poultry farming means raising chickens for eggs or meat. Keep sheds clean, give good feed and water, and protect birds from diseases for better profits.

Key Practices

  • Maintain optimal temperature and ventilation in sheds.
  • Provide clean water and balanced commercial feed.
  • Follow vaccination schedule; limit visitors (biosecurity).
  • Keep litter dry and manage ammonia levels.
  • Segregate age groups; implement all-in/all-out systems.
