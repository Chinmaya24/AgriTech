Dairy Farming

Healthy cows, quality feed, and hygienic milking practices for top yields.

Overview

Dairy farming means keeping cows or buffaloes healthy and stress-free so they give good milk. Clean sheds, proper feed, and neat milking help farmers earn better.

Key Practices

  • Balanced ration with adequate energy, protein, and minerals.
  • Clean housing with proper bedding and ventilation.
  • Routine health checks, vaccinations, and deworming.
  • Milking hygiene: clean teats, sanitized equipment, quick cooling.
  • Record-keeping for yield, breeding, and health events.
