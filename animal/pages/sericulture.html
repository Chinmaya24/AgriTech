Sericulture (Silkworm Rearing)

Grow mulberry, rear silkworms carefully, and harvest quality cocoons.

Overview

Sericulture means growing mulberry and rearing silkworms to make silk. Keep rooms clean, feed fresh leaves, and manage heat and humidity carefully.

Key Practices

  • Maintain ideal temperature and humidity in rearing houses.
  • Feed fresh, pesticide-free mulberry leaves at stages.
  • Keep trays and rooms clean to prevent diseases.
  • Provide proper mountages for cocoon formation.
  • Grade and dry cocoons carefully after harvest.
