Apiculture (Beekeeping)

Bee-friendly practices that support pollination and quality honey.

Overview

Apiculture means keeping bee colonies safely to get honey and wax. Place hives where flowers are available and protect bees from heat and pesticides.

Key Practices

  • Place hives near diverse flowering plants; ensure water availability.
  • Inspect hives regularly for queen status and pests like Varroa.
  • Provide shade and wind protection; avoid pesticide exposure.
  • Harvest honey hygienically and leave enough stores for bees.
  • Use protective gear and calm handling techniques.
